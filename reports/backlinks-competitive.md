## 7. Disavow / Toxic Links

Risk level: **Low** today. Spam-directory backlinks are common across the open web; Google ignores them automatically since 2016. **Do NOT create a disavow file proactively.** If GSC issues a manual action or shows spam links rising rapidly, then disavow the worst offenders (factmags.com, the alltopleveldomains.space cluster, the .cv / .monster / .xyz cluster).
