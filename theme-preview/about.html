Trust + Compliance

Verification you can check, not just claim.

Open EB5 operates under federal designation and ongoing oversight. We publish the verifiers our investors actually need.

USCIS Designation

Open EB5 is a USCIS-designated EB-5 Regional Center under the EB-5 Reform and Integrity Act of 2022. Designation ID is publicly verifiable through the official USCIS Regional Center search.

SEC Compliance

All offerings are structured under SEC Regulation D 506(c). Each project ships with a Private Placement Memorandum (PPM) and subscription agreements reviewed by securities counsel.

AML / KYC Protocols

Source-of-funds verification follows BSA standards. OFAC and politically-exposed-person screening on every investor. We do not accept any investor whose funds cannot be sourced.

Investor Privacy

Investor data is stored encrypted at rest, transmitted over TLS, and never sold. PII-bearing documents (passport, financial records) are kept in access-controlled folders with audit logs.

Independent Immigration Counsel

Every Open EB5 investor retains independent U.S. immigration counsel — separate from Open EB5 — for the I-526E and I-829 filings. We refer to several established firms, but the attorney-client relationship is yours alone.

This separation is required by the U.S. Bar and protects you in any potential conflict of interest.
